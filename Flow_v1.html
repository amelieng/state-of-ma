Most Bostonians aren't building wealth —
they're paying someone else's mortgage.

68% of Boston residents rent. This chapter shows the long-run shift from ownership to renting, tracks rent burden as a share of income over time, and makes the wealth transfer visible: every month of rent is equity someone else accumulates.

Data needed for this chapter:
  • Renter vs. owner household counts over time — homeownership_boston_acs.csv (columns: renter_occupied_units, owner_occupied_units)
  • Median gross rent as % of median household income — homeownership_summary.csv (column: rent_burden_pct_of_income)
  • Key stat: 68% of Boston residents rent (ACS B25003, most recent year)
